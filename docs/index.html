QMES Foundation

QMES was founded by a group of passionate engineers from Queen Mary, who shared a common interest and motivation in electronics, programming, and robotics. Our society has 3 key goals:
To host weekly electronics-related activities for other STEM students, or anyone with a fascination with electronics.
Participate in at least 3 electronics-related competitions per year.
Provide electronics outreach and education.
